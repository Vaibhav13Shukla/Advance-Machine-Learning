Welcome to the Student Exam Performance Predictor

This application helps predict students' Math exam scores based on various factors such as gender, race/ethnicity, parental education, lunch type, and test preparation course completion.

Go to Prediction Form

How it works

  • Fill in the details in the form provided.
  • Click on the "Predict Your Maths Score" button.
  • Get the predicted score based on the inputs provided.

About the Application

This application uses machine learning algorithms to predict a student's Math score based on a variety of factors. It helps students understand the impact of their choices on their academic performance.
